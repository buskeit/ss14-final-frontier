The Aurelian Reach is an unknown region of space, located in the Andromeda Galaxy. It was first discovered in 2326, when a small group of NanoTrasen scientists researched an anomaly, that appeared in the Alpha Centaury system. The ships got somehow sucked into some kind of vortex which led it to the region, which is now known as the Aurelian Reach. Being thousands of light years away from home and from NanoTrasen, the original team has started to settle in their new home, form their own faction and contiue researching both, the Reach as well as working on a way to get home.

The Aurelian Reach is far away from home, located in the Andromeda Galaxy. This makes it impossible for humans, to reach it in conventional ways, due to the lack of technology for intergalactic travel. The few newcomers, that have arrived, ever since the original team made it into the reach, are mostly other scientists and spacefarers, that made contact with the anamoly, which apparently appeared in other regions of space. A gravitational barrier within the reach makes it also very hard to leave. If you fly a ship far in any direction you will eventually hit the barrier and be pushed back towards the center.

Until the colonists of the Aurelian Reach can find a way to expand or disable the barrier they are trapped in this area of space.
New arrivals to the sector, will most likely make first contact with Aurelia Station, which is located in a central area of the sector, near to where most new arrivals seem to appear. It is recommend, to seek out the station's commanding officer in order to get more information, a job and a warm meal. While it is understandble, that you miss your home and that you wish to return to your loved ones, this region of space also offers incredible new opportunities, an entire new life indeed. It is up to you, what you will make of it.
