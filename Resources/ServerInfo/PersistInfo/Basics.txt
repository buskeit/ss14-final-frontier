The Aurelian Reach is a region located in another galaxy. In fact, it is found in the Andromeda Galaxy, one of the galaxies, neighboring the Milky Way, where the Sol System and Earth are located. Its discovery was not intended and an accident. It happened, when a group or NanoTrasen Researchers conducted research of an anomaly, found in the Alpha Centaury system. For reasons unknown, the station and several of the ships, that were near the anamaly, got sucked into it and after a short period of travel, appeared in the Andromeda Galaxy, in what has now become the Aurelian Reach. Stranded alone and far away from home, the options were limited and as such the colonization of the region was started in order to ensure survival. A new faction, the Aurelian League was formed, having the now renamed Aurelia Station as its hub. Without any corporations or any other form of government, Aurelia Station and the Aurelian League are the only pillars of security and stability in the system. A new human culture, a new government, without any of the known mega corporations was born.

Ever since the original group of researchers has been travelling to the anomaly to the Aurelian Reach, few others followed. Either on purpose, by heading towards the anomaly, to find out what happened to the researchers, to follow their steps, or by accident, when the anomaly appeared near their ships. Whatever the reason, the fate is always the same: Life in the Aurelian Reach. You are one of these colonists. A traveler, that on purpose or not, is now stranded far away from home and loved ones. What you will do about it, depends entirely on you. Work with the Aurelian Reach or maybe start your own faction or corporation and follow your own goals. The choice is yours. Welcome to the Aurelian Reach.
