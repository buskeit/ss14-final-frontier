As a colonist in the Aurelian Reach, you are far away from home and from the local governments, you know. There are no human governments nor any mega corporations, you are used to see dominating back home. In this area, located far away from home, in the Andromeda Galaxy, everything is different. Aurelia Station is run by humans and aliens alike, who have formed the Aurelian League. A peaceful, research and exploration oriented faction, that is in a way, the only official government in the region. It is providing administrative infrastructure, resources and security to the Aurelian Reach.

However, the sector is too big, with too many stations, to be controlled by a single faction. As such, other factions, small and big, have emerged in the Aurelian Reach, ranging from small ship or shuttle crews, to big corporations, with own ships and stations. Everybody is able to request the creation of a faction or corporation, as long as you have a group of people backing you up, supporting your endeavor. Where will you serve? What will you do? You cant be forced to work for the Aurelian League or for anybody. The choice is yours. Will you work with the Aurelian League or will you look for your own way? Maybe you will be an outlaw or smuggler, trying the security systems and the laws of the new and fledging colony? There are no limits, other than your imagination.

Colonists are free to associate and work with multiple factions, with exception of the Aurelian League, which is an official government faction and as such, does not allow to work with other factions in any official role. However, you can still associate with these factions or contract them, for official business if needed.
